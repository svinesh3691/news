55 of 57 newly-elected RS members are crorepatis
As many as 55 of the 57 newly-elected​ Rajya Sabha members are crorepatis, an Association for Democratic Reforms survey found. Praful Patel (over ₹252 crore) and Kapil Sibal (over ₹212 crore) were the MPs with the highest assets. The survey also found that 23% of the MPs were linked to criminal cases, including the 12% involved in serious criminal cases.

Shastri desperate to become coach: Gambhir
Amid the controversy between Ravi Shastri and Sourav Ganguly over the Indian team's head coach position, Gautam Gambhir said, "I think it was Shastri’s desperation to become the coach that led him to make such statements". "I feel that Anil Kumble is best man for the job and he has been, deservingly, rewarded with the coveted position", Gambhir added.

Supreme Court hears plea to end triple talaq
The Supreme Court on Wednesday heard a plea that sought to end the practice of Muslim men divorcing their wives by saying "talaq" thrice. The court said it will examine how far it can interfere in Muslim laws "within the framework of the Constitution". It also asked the government for its views on whether intervening would violate Muslims' fundamental rights.

749 cr assets attached in YSRC chief graft case
The Enforcement Directorate (ED) on Wednesday attached properties worth ₹749 crore belonging to YSR Congress president YS Jaganmohan Reddy and his wife in a graft case under the Prevention of Money Laundering Act. The ED said Reddy received bribes in the guise of investments in his companies, for favours granted by the previous YS Rajasekhar Reddy led-Andhra Pradesh government.
